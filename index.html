Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

100% Local Intelligence

Seeky is built on the principle of absolute data sovereignty. Your data belongs to you and never leaves your machine.

1. Information Collected

Seeky does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to the developer or any third-party servers.

  • Local Metadata: Your filenames and content stay on your local hardware.
  • No Cloud Sync: There is no cloud component to Seeky.

2. No Cloud Transmission

Seeky does not integrate with external AI cloud providers. All operations are processed entirely on your local CPU/GPU using local resources only.

3. Security & Permissions

Seeky only accesses folders you explicitly choose to link. Network access is restricted to application updates only—no user content is ever transmitted.
